How to Play Shooter

Objective: Click on food items as they fall! Avoid clicking on baby pets!

How to Play:

  • Click "Start Game" to begin
  • Click on food items to earn 1 point each
  • Avoid clicking on baby pets - they cost you 1 point!
  • Every 5 points earns you $8
  • Game lasts 60 seconds
  • Items move faster every 10 seconds

Pet Effects: After the game, your pet loses 10 sleep and 10 hunger, but gains 15 experience.
