How to Play Hurdles

Objective: Jump over walls and spikes to survive as long as possible!

Controls:

  • Press SPACE or click to make your pet jump
  • Hold to jump higher

Rewards:

  • Earn $10 every 30 seconds
  • The game speeds up every 5 seconds

Game Over: Hit a wall or spike to end the game

Note: Your pet will lose 10 sleep and 15 hunger after each game
