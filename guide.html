Game Guide

Welcome to Digital Pet!

Digital Pet is a desktop pet simulation game where you care for your virtual companion. Feed them, play with them, and watch them grow!

Getting Started

  1. Purchase an egg from the Shop (Eggs tab)
  2. Click the egg 10 times to hatch it
  3. Name your pet when prompted
  4. Start caring for your pet by feeding, playing, and keeping them healthy!

Basic Concepts

  • Stats: Your pet has four main stats: Health, Hunger, Happiness, and Rest
  • Money: You earn $50 every 1.5 minutes automatically
  • Shop: Buy food, medicine, toys, games, and eggs
  • Games: Play minigames to earn money and affect your pet's stats
  • Evolution: Pets can evolve and change forms through experience

Pet States

  • Normal: Your pet's default state when all needs are met
  • Sleeping: Put your pet to sleep via Actions menu. While sleeping:
    • Rest increases (+5 every 45 seconds)
    • Health increases (+5 every 4 minutes)
    • Rest decay stops
    • Hunger and Happiness still decay
  • Exercising: Put your pet to exercise via Actions menu. Increases experience over time
  • Sick: Your pet has a chance to get sick every 5 minutes. When sick:
    • Health decreases (-10 every 1.5 minutes)
    • Feed medicine to cure them
  • Dead: If health reaches 0, your pet dies. You'll need to buy a new egg

Waste System

Your pet produces waste over time (every 2.5 minutes). Every 5 minutes, there's a chance your pet will get sick. The more waste that appears on screen, the higher the chance of getting sick:

  • Waste spawn: Every 2.5 minutes, your pet leaves waste on the screen
  • Base sickness chance: 15% every 5 minutes
  • Additional chance: +10% for each waste item on screen
  • Example: With 2 waste items on screen, the chance is 15% + (2 √ó 10%) = 35%

Clean up waste by clicking on it in the pet window to reduce the sickness chance!

Experience & Evolution

Your pet gains experience from:

  • Playing games
  • Exercising
  • Using certain toys
  • Eating food
  • Petting your pet
  • Moving your pet around
  • Passive gain over time (every 3 minutes)

Each pet can evolve 4 times, changing forms at specific experience thresholds:

  • Stage 1 ‚Üí Stage 2: Reach 250 experience
  • Stage 2 ‚Üí Stage 3: Reach 400 experience
  • Stage 3 ‚Üí Stage 4: Reach 600 experience
  • Stage 4: Final form (no further evolution)

When your pet evolves, experience resets to 0 and the experience threshold increases for the next evolution. Evolution increases the pet's value when selling.

Selling Your Pet

You can sell your pet in the Sell tab. The base price depends on the pet type:

  • Basic pets: Base price $250, plus 20% per evolution stage
  • Intermediate pets: Base price $350, plus 20% per evolution stage

Examples (Basic pets):

  • Stage 1 (no evolution): $250
  • Stage 2 (1 evolution): $300
  • Stage 3 (2 evolutions): $360

Examples (Intermediate pets):

  • Stage 1 (no evolution): $350
  • Stage 2 (1 evolution): $420
  • Stage 3 (2 evolutions): $504

Warning: Selling your pet will remove it from your game. You'll need to buy a new egg to get another pet.

  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the colored quadrants in the same order

  • Lose: $3 per match made, +5 happiness, -5 sleep, -5 hunger, +7 experience

Egg Mechanics

  • You can only have one egg at a time
  • Once you have an egg, you cannot buy another until you hatch or sell your current pet
  • Click the egg 10 times in the pet window to hatch it
  • After hatching, you'll be prompted to name your pet
  • Once hatched, your pet's stats will start decaying

Miscellaneous

Money System

  • You earn $50 every 1.5 minutes automatically
  • Play minigames to earn additional money
  • Sell your pet to get money (but you'll lose your pet!)
  • Starting money: $400

Pet Naming

When you hatch an egg, you'll be prompted to name your pet. The pet's name will appear in the window title and can be used to identify your pet.

Actions Menu

Use the Actions menu (top menu bar) to:

  • Sleep/Wake Up: Put your pet to sleep or wake them up
  • Exercise/Stop Exercise: Start or stop exercising your pet

Note: Actions are only available after your egg has hatched.

Windows

  • Pet Window: Your main pet display - drag your pet around, feed them, clean waste
  • Shop: Buy items, games, and eggs
  • Games: Access your purchased minigames
  • Settings: Test features and reset your game
  • Guide: This window - helpful information about the game

Save System

Your game progress is automatically saved:

  • Pet stats and state
  • Money and purchased items
  • Pet name and evolution stage
  • Active toys and their timers

Your save file persists even when you close the game. Use the Settings menu to reset your game if needed.

Tips & Tricks

  • Keep your pet's stats above 0 to prevent health decay
  • Put your pet to sleep regularly to restore health and rest
  • Use toys strategically - they stack, so multiple toys can be active at once
  • Play games to earn money while also affecting your pet's stats
  • Clean up waste regularly to prevent sickness
  • Exercise your pet to gain experience for evolution
