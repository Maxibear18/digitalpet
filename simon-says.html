How to Play Simon Says

Objective

Watch the sequence of colors shown by your pet, then repeat it by clicking the colored quadrants in the same order.

How to Play

  1. Click "Start Game" to begin
  2. Watch as the colored quadrants light up in a sequence
  3. Click the quadrants in the same order you saw them
  4. Each round adds one more color to the sequence
  5. Get it wrong and the game ends!

Rewards

  • Money: $2 for each round you complete successfully
  • On Game Over: Your pet will lose 5 hunger and 5 rest, but gain 5 happiness

Tips

  • Pay close attention to the sequence - it gets faster!
  • Your pet will celebrate when you complete a round correctly
  • Try to beat your high score!
