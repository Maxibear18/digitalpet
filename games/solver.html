How to Play Solver

Objective: Solve math problems as quickly as you can!

How to Play:

  • Click "Start Game" to begin
  • You have 15 seconds to solve each problem
  • Enter your answer and click "Submit"
  • Every 3 correct answers earns you $12
  • Problems get harder every 3 correct answers
  • You have 3 mistakes before the game ends

Game Over: The game ends when you run out of time, answer incorrectly 3 times, or answer incorrectly.

Pet Effects: When you lose, your pet gains 10 happiness and 10 experience, but loses 10 sleep and 5 hunger.
