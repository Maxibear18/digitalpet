How to Play Memory Match

Objective

Flip cards to find matching pairs of pets and food. Match all pairs to win!

How to Play

  1. Click "Start Game" to begin
  2. Click a card to flip it over
  3. Click another card to try to find a match
  4. If they match, they stay flipped. If not, they flip back
  5. Match all pairs before making 5 mistakes!

Rules

  • You can only make 5 mistakes before losing
  • A mistake is when you flip two cards that don't match
  • Try to remember where cards are located

Rewards

  • Win: $50, +10 happiness, -5 sleep, -5 hunger, +15 experience
  • Lose: $3 per match made, +5 happiness, -5 sleep, -5 hunger, +7 experience

Tips

  • Pay attention to the positions of cards you've seen
  • Try to remember where matching pairs are
  • Don't rush - take your time to think
