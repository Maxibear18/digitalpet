How to Play Snake

Objective: Control your pet snake and collect food to grow longer!

How to Play:

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move your pet
  • Collect food items to grow longer
  • Every 3 pets collected earns you $6
  • Don't hit the walls or yourself - game over!

Pet Effects: After the game, your pet gains 10 experience and 10 happiness, but loses 10 sleep and 5 hunger.
