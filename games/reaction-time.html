How to Play Reaction Time

Objective

Press the SPACEBAR to stop the timer at exactly 10 seconds. The closer you get, the more money you earn!

How to Play

  1. Click "Start Game" to begin
  2. Press SPACEBAR to start the timer
  3. Watch the timer count up
  4. Press SPACEBAR again when you think it's at exactly 10 seconds
  5. See how close you got!

Rewards

  • Exactly 10.00 seconds: $55
  • 0.1 or 0.2 seconds off: $30 (halved if over 10s)
  • 0.3 seconds off: $25 (halved if over 10s)
  • 0.5 seconds off: $20 (halved if over 10s)
  • 0.7 seconds off: $15 (halved if over 10s)
  • 1.0 second off: $10 (halved if over 10s)
  • More than 1 second off: $0

Note: Rewards are halved if you go over 10 seconds (e.g., 0.3s over = $12 instead of $25)

Pet Effects

  • Each game: -5 sleep, -5 hunger, +5 experience, +5 happiness

Tips

  • Focus on the timer and try to anticipate when it will hit 10 seconds
  • Practice your timing - it gets easier with experience!
  • Don't rush - take your time to get the perfect timing
