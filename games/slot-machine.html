How to Play Slot Machine

Objective: Match food symbols on the middle row to win money for your pet!

How to Play:

  • Choose your bet ($0-$100)
  • Click "Spin" to roll the reels
  • Only the middle row of food matters — line up 2 or 3 of the same snack to score rewards

Payouts (middle row only):

  • Two of a kind: Cherries x1.1, Riceball x1.2, Sandwich x1.3, Meat x1.5, Cake x1.7
  • Three of a kind: Cherries x1.3, Riceball x1.5, Sandwich x1.7, Meat x2.0, Cake x2.5
  • No match on the middle row: you lose your bet

Pet Effects: Every 3 spins your pet loses 5 hunger and 5 sleep but gains 5 happiness and 5 experience.

Celebrations: Matching wins make your pet pop out in a bubble to cheer you on!
